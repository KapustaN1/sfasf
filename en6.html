Our residents
SPOK "Tirekh"
Since 2006, the agricultural consumer cooperative "Tirekh" has been one of the first in the Republic to be engaged in the field of environmental entrepreneurship. Currently, 20-25 tons of berries are harvested annually. About 20 types of products are produced - jams, syrups, berry sauces, frozen berries. The products are sold mainly within the republic through trade organizations. Small batches are sent as orders are received to the central regions of Russia and to foreign countries.
LLC "Dary Yakutia"
At present, MIP LLC "Dary Yakutia" is engaged in the production of edible fish-bone meal from whitefish species. It is used to produce an encapsulated dietary supplement under the registered trademark "Calcium-Sigostin".
Mission of the project: To offer a high-quality, mass-produced, healthy product that meets all standards.
The special significance of the proposed food product with high biological activity lies in the primary and secondary prevention of diseases associated with calcium-phosphorus metabolism disorders.
