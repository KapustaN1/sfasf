Technopark "Yakutia"
State Autonomous Institution "Technopark "Yakutia"»
created by the Order of the President of the Republic of Sakha (Yakutia) No. 998-RP dated 28.12.11.
The Technopark, as an object of the innovative infrastructure of the republic, is designed to ensure the territorial concentration of financial and intellectual resources to accelerate the innovative development of economic sectors.
