Our Residents
EGOPLAST LLC Yakutsk plant EGOPLAST
is a manufacturer of polyethylene pipes in the Republic of Sakha (Yakutia). The plant produces pipes of various diameters from DN20mm to DN630mm from high-quality, environmentally friendly raw materials PE 100, PE 100 pipes are frost-resistant and corrosion-resistant. The products of the Egoplast plant are used in the installation of water supply, gas supply, sewerage systems, for the heating system of the PE RT, irrigation and irrigation of the garden, as a casing for cables when crossing obstacles, etc.
LLC "SUNTARENERGO"
The company is engaged in the production of consumer goods from zeolite and building materials (foam zeolite). The company's products are penozeolite, zeolite tuff No. 1, 2, 3, shoe deodorants, ZeoFresh refrigerator deodorant-dryer, ZeoCat cat litter. It was organized in February 1989 on the initiative of the Chairman of the Suntarsky RIC Vladimir Mikhailovich Chlenov, the Institute of Geological Sciences of the Siberian Branch of the Russian Academy of Sciences in Yakutsk and the discoverer of the Khongurinsky zeolite deposit, Doctor of Geological and Mineralogical Sciences K.E. Kolodeznikov for the pilot development of zeolites. The company is faced with the task of providing zeolite raw materials to scientific researchers and for practical use in the sectors of the national economy of the republic of the zeolite mineral of the Honguruu deposit.
